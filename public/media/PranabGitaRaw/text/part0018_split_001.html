PARAM GURUDEV SRIMAT YOGIRAJ

 

Shyamacharan Lahiri Mahasaya

 

 

O Yogiraj! Please take my prostrations.

I beg you to have mercy and take me across the ocean of ignorance

Swami Pranabananda Giri Maharaj [is]

Your disciple, my guru. Today

I take the floral-wreath of his holy Pranab Gita

And offer it to you as a reverent homage, with my head bowed

 

May this offering be sanctified. I hold it on my head

For sadhus, devotees, yogis in the battlefield of sadhana

Let there be victory; and Supreme Peace, by your grace

May It be attained; such is my petition at your feet

 

O Yogiraj! Please take my prostrations!

 

Sri Jnanendranath Devasharmanah

(Mukhopadhyasya)
