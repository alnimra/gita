RAMENDRA NATH MUKERJEE                              
 

PRANAB GITA BHABAN


Dated 15, May 2000

Dear Ned,

Further to your letter dated 5, April 2000, I send herewith typed copies of short life sketch of Sree Jnanendranath Mukhopadyay and his spiritual aspirations.


I have translated the Bengali text I write earlier. You are at liberty to abridge it, make corrections, re-model it to suit your website library. You may send your comments.


The English version of Pranab Gita chapter by chapter and the biography of Swami Pranabanand Guru are very much encouraging to me. I am, however, perturbed to learn that B is likely to leave the U.S.A and return if A is un-certain. I pray, that you find some other good soul to help you in the project conceived. Kindly let me know the developments.


I have gone through the material concerning Katyayani Peeth and noted with pleasure the comments made by Vidyanandaji in respect of PRANAB GITA. I am happy to visualise that the book will be available in English through the sole efforts of Ned Waller of U.S.A. Let him have the blessing of Swamijee and Jnan Maharaj. I hope you are listening.


Eagerly awaiting for the next mail from you.


With my best wishes to you and the Yoga Niketan members.

Yours sincerely

R Mukherjee

15/05/2000
