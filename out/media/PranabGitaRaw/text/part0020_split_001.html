ATHA MANGALACHARANAM

 

____________________

 

jnAtvA sarvarahasyamAtma vishayaṁ sarvAtmajohpi svayaṁ

yah kAruNyavashvena tAvadalikhadvaiyAsikeeṁ bharateem

taṁ natvakhiladevapujita padaṁ sarvArthasiddhi pradaṁ

geetAyah khalu yogashAstravihitaṁ vyAkhyAnamAkhyAyate – 1

 

yasmAdasya guNashrayasya jagatah sṛSTisthitibhraṁshanaṁ

yashchaikehpi vichitrakaushalamayo dharte vibhinnaṁ vapuh

antaryaṁ tamasah paraṁ hi purushaṁ pashyanti ruddhendriyA

yogeendrah praNamamyahaṁ tamasakṛin nihshreyasa prAptaye – 2

 

geete jnAnamayi tvameva kṛpayA madbodhagamyA bhava

vANI nṛtyatu me tathaiva sarasA jihvagrabhAge sadA

yattehadhyAtmamayaṁ rahasyamatulaṁ prakhyApya kalyANadaṁ

brahmAnandarasaih svabhaktimadhuraih sincheyamajnAnaham – 3

 

ajnAnatimiraNdhasya jnAnAnjanashalAkayA

chakshurunmeelitaṁ yena mamAnukampayaiva cha

akhandamandalAkAraṁ vyaptaṁ yena charAcharaṁ

tatpadaṁ darshitah chaiva tasmai shreeguruve namah – 4

 

OM

 

Pranabananda Swami
